# Privacy Policy for Pax

**Last Updated: December 16, 2024**

## Introduction

Welcome to Pax, a blockchain-powered micro-task platform that enables organizations to create tasks and reward participants with cryptocurrency tokens. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Pax is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This policy applies to all users of the Pax mobile application, including task participants and organizations creating tasks.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you create an account with Pax, we collect:
- **Display name** from your Google account
- **Email address** associated with your Google account
- **Profile picture** from your Google account (if provided)
- **Phone number** (if you choose to provide it)
- **Gender** information
- **Country/location** information
- **Date of birth** (for age verification purposes)

### 2. Authentication Data
- **Google Sign-In credentials** and authentication tokens
- **Firebase Authentication** user ID and session data
- **Privy wallet service** authentication for blockchain account management

### 3. Task and Activity Data
- **Task completion information** including timestamps and results
- **Screening data** for task eligibility verification
- **Task performance metrics** and completion history
- **Reward and payment history** including cryptocurrency transactions
- **Achievement progress** and badge information

### 4. Financial and Blockchain Data
- **Cryptocurrency wallet addresses** (smart contract accounts)
- **Payment method information** for withdrawals (bank accounts, mobile money details)
- **Transaction history** including rewards received and withdrawals made
- **Token balances** across supported cryptocurrencies (CUSD, Good Dollar, USDT, USDC)
- **Blockchain transaction hashes** and smart contract interactions

### 5. Technical and Usage Data
- **Device information** including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
- **App usage analytics** including feature usage, session duration, and user interactions
- **Performance data** through Firebase Crashlytics for app stability monitoring
- **Network connectivity** information for offline functionality
- **Push notification tokens** for Firebase Cloud Messaging

### 6. Location Data
- **Country-level location** for compliance and regulatory purposes
- **Network-based location** for security and fraud prevention
- We do **not** collect precise GPS location data

### 7. Third-Party Identity Verification
- **Good Dollar identity verification** data and timestamps
- **Identity verification status** and expiry dates
- **Verification badges** and achievement data

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Platform Functions
- **Account Management**: Creating and maintaining your user account and profile
- **Task Assignment**: Matching you with suitable micro-tasks based on your profile and eligibility
- **Reward Distribution**: Processing cryptocurrency payments through smart contracts
- **Achievement Tracking**: Monitoring progress and awarding badges for milestones
- **Withdrawal Processing**: Facilitating transfers to your linked payment methods

### Security and Compliance
- **Identity Verification**: Ensuring compliance with financial regulations and preventing fraud
- **Screening Processes**: Verifying participant eligibility for specific tasks
- **Transaction Security**: Using cryptographic signatures for secure blockchain operations
- **Account Protection**: Monitoring for suspicious activity and unauthorized access

### Service Improvement
- **Analytics and Insights**: Understanding user behavior to improve the platform
- **Performance Monitoring**: Identifying and fixing technical issues through crash reporting
- **Feature Development**: Using aggregated data to develop new features and improvements
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring reliable task completion and reward distribution

### Communication
- **Push Notifications**: Sending updates about new tasks, rewards, and platform announcements
- **Support Services**: Providing customer support and technical assistance
- **Regulatory Communications**: Sending required disclosures and compliance information

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction, subject to the same privacy protections.

## Data Security and Protection

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using industry-standard encryption
- **Secure Storage**: Personal data is stored in Google Cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit who can view and modify user data
- **Smart Contract Security**: Our blockchain contracts follow OpenZeppelin security standards

### Blockchain Security Features
- **Account Abstraction**: Gasless transactions through secure smart contract wallets
- **Cryptographic Signatures**: EIP-712 signatures for secure transaction authorization
- **Multi-layer Verification**: Multiple security checks for sensitive operations

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data may be deleted after extended periods of inactivity
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations
- **Blockchain Data**: On-chain transactions are permanent and cannot be deleted
